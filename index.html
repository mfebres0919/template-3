Got Questions?

Frequently
Asked

Everything you need to know before booking your first appointment.

How long does a wig install take?+

Most installs take between 1.5 to 3 hours depending on the style and technique. Lace melts and detailed baby hair work may take a bit longer.

Do I need to bring my own wig?+

Yes! We install your wig — we don't supply one. Please arrive with a clean, pre-plucked, and pre-bleached wig ready to go.

What's the difference between a frontal and closure?+

A frontal runs ear to ear giving more styling versatility. A closure is smaller (usually 4x4 or 5x5) and sits in the middle — great for natural-looking parts.

How long will my install last?+

With proper care, an install typically lasts 2–4 weeks. We recommend sleeping with a satin bonnet and avoiding excess moisture at the roots.

Is a deposit required to book?+

Yes — a $25 non-refundable deposit is required to secure your appointment. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance.

Can I get my wig styled the same day?+

Absolutely! Just let us know when booking so we can schedule enough time. We do cuts, curls, flat iron, and more.

What if I need to reschedule?+

You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment at no charge. Use the link in your confirmation email or contact us directly.
